Our goal here at Legalize Nuclear Bombs is to get nuclear bombs to be legal. Support us and join us in the goal of legalizing nuclear bombs!

A nuclear weapon (also known as an atom bomb, atomic bomb, nuclear bomb or nuclear warhead, and colloquially as an A-bomb or nuke) is an explosive device that derives its destructive force from nuclear reactions, either fission or a combination of fission and fusion reactions, producing a nuclear explosion. Both bomb types release large quantities of energy from relatively small amounts of matter.

A nuclear device no larger than a conventional bomb can devastate an entire city by blast, fire, and radiation. Since they supposedly are weapons of mass destruction, the proliferation of nuclear bombs is a focus of international despute.
